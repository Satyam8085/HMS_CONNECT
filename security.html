🔐 Security & Compliance

We prioritize data protection, privacy, and regulatory compliance at every level of our system.

📄 HIPAA & NABH Compliance

Adheres to international and Indian standards for healthcare data privacy, such as HIPAA and NABH accreditation.

🔍 Audit Trails & Activity Logs

Tracks all access to sensitive information with detailed logs for investigation and monitoring.

🛡️ Data Encryption

Encrypts patient data using AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit for maximum security.

👮 Role-Based Access Control

Restricts access by department, role, and privilege to minimize risk and enforce accountability.

📆 Backup & Disaster Recovery

Daily backups with real-time syncing and geo-redundant storage ensure data resilience and rapid recovery.

🔔 Real-time Security Alerts

Alerts triggered for unauthorized access attempts or data anomalies via SMS/email and admin dashboard.
